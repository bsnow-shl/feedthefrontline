OUR SIMPLE GOAL

We are currently experiencing a pandemic that is putting tremendous strain on our health care facilities.  The daily news continually mentions equipment & protection material shortages.  Not much has been said about the front line medical teams that are facing COVID-19 head on.  We want to not only support these teams but thank them for risking their health to save ours.  Working multiple shifts in a row due to higher patient volumes can takes it toll.  Let's help give them the strength to keep fighting.  Let's Feed The Front Line.
